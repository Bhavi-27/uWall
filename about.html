About uWall

uWall is a digital platform designed for the students of Chitkara University Punjab. It serves as a virtual wall where students can relive their cherished memories, celebrate their achievements in various fests, and showcase the trophies they have won in competitions.

Our mission is to create a vibrant community where students can share their experiences and connect with one another through the memories captured in photos and stories. Whether it's a fest, a competition, or a trophy showcase, uWall is here to celebrate every moment.

Join us in preserving the legacy of Chitkara University Punjab and be a part of our growing digital wall!
